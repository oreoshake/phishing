Kealakehe High School — Bell Schedules

Five Day Weeks
  • Students’ day is from 8:15 to 3:13 on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday.  Students are released at 2:10 on Wednesdays.
  • Teachers’ Day is from 8:14 to 3:14 everyday.
  • NOTE TO TEACHERS: On Wednesday and Thursday, the prep period is scheduled for 51 minutes and an additional 32 minutes for an admin conferencing period (added juxtaposed to the end of the prep period). This period is used as needed by an administrator to conference with certificated personnel.
Modified Weeks (Four Day Weeks)
  • Students\\' day is from 9:00 to 3:14 everyday of the four day week.
  • Teachers\\' day is from 8:10 to 3:10 for those who have morning prep and/or PLC meetings OR from 8:55 to 3:55 for those with afternoon prep and/or PLC meetings.
  • NOTE TO TEACHERS: for teachers scheduled for a prep period within the students\\' day, the prep period is scheduled for 55 minutes and a 35 minute admin conference period is added juxtaposed to the end of the prep period.  This period is used as needed by an administrator to conference with certificated personnel.
